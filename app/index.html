Usability Tests

User Research / Usability Test Design / Usability Test Moderation

Gain a clear, focused picture of how new users do with Vegas Movie Studio, so that we can plan improvements based on realistic experiences.

Using Steve Krug’s Rocket Surgery Made Easy as a guide, I planned and conducted the first ever usability tests for the company. The tests examined three participants’ ability to do simple editing tasks such as removing unwanted parts from a video, adding text, and organizing the order of clips in a project. The tests were streamed live to a room for team members to observe, and were followed up by a team discussion to determine next actions to fix the biggest problems.

The results showed that many assumptions of the effectiveness of basic UI were wrong. Not a single participant discovered or understood basic concepts of how to move the cursor, what was represented in the Timeline, or that elements in the Timeline were manipulatable.
